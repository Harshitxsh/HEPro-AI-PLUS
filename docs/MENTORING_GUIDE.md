# Mentoring Strategy Guide

## Tactical Recommendations for Student Clusters

This document outlines the strategic mentoring interventions recommended for each student segment identified through unsupervised machine learning. It serves as a tactical guide for personalized student support.

---

## 🟢 Group: Balanced & Consistent

### Priority: Low

* **Primary Focus**: Academic enrichment and leadership development.
* **Strategic Action**: Enroll in advanced modules and peer-mentoring roles. These students demonstrate high stability and require minimal intervention beyond routine monitoring.

## 🟡 Group: High-Achievers (Stressed Out)

### Priority: Medium

* **Primary Focus**: Wellness management and operational efficiency.
* **Strategic Action**: Implement stress-reduction protocols and time-management coaching. Efforts should focus on optimizing productivity without further increasing academic pressure.

## 🟠 Group: The Lost & Confused

### Priority: High

* **Primary Focus**: Vocational clarity and goal alignment.
* **Strategic Action**: Conduct comprehensive career counseling and industry orientation. Addressing the lack of direction is critical to restoring academic motivation.

## 🔴 Group: High-Risk (Needs Help Now)

### Priority: Immediate

* **Primary Focus**: Crisis stabilization and academic salvage.
* **Strategic Action**: Initiation of an immediate multi-disciplinary intervention. Identify underlying causes of disengagement—whether academic, psychological, or situational—to prevent attrition.
